My focus areas

The following are the areas I believe need immediate attention
if we are going to take Kwa Ruben to the next level

Fresh and Clean Water

Maji ni Uhai na right yetu sote. I'll ensure this becomes a reality

Youth and Sports

Rube ni hub ya talents. Soccer, Basketball, Volleyball, Athletics etc. My leadership will ensure there is enough resources for sports

Business

We need access to business loans for youth and women in Mukuru. I'll set a fund aside to act as collateral so that serious individuals can access banl loans

Security and Justice

We'll follow all extra-judicial killings to their final conclusion. Where I smell injustice, I'll put all the necessary ward resources to assist

Gender violence

I'll work with the relevant organizations and agencies to ensure that our children are safe from sexual predators.

Infrastructure

Current leadership has failed to build or even maintain existing roads, trenches, and sports groungs because all they do is steal. This will not happen under my watch
